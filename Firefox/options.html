Import List

You may need to do a few things in order to view the extension options page in its entirety. Rather than having to look at the small box.

The link to the options page is moz-extension://{Internal UUID}/options.html

To get Internal UUID you'll need to go to "Add-ons and themes" -> gear wheel under the search -> "Debug Add-ons" -> and search for Internal UUID in Domain Blocker.

Exmaple: moz-extension://64c9258d-1a3b-4bff-b8ef-1b647aea933e/options.html
