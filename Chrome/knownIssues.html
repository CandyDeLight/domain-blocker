Known Issues

1.) If you are signed into twitter, it does not seem to block twitter.com for some reason.

2.) Depending on how the tab is opened and or where the domain was blocked, it may or may not display the correct URL.

3.) Trying to block a website with its path/route "exmaple.com/aboutus" doesn't seem to work on Firefox.

4.) Your list can grow fairly quickly. Depending on the size, it may take some time for options.html/HOME tab to display your list.
